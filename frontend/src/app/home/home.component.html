Add an extra layer of security to your accounts

TOTP are temporary codes that you can use as a second factor of authentication to secure your accounts (gmail, facebook, twitter, github, etc ...). It's like a SMS code, but it's more secure and more convenient !

A TOTP code is only valid for 30 seconds. Each time you log in to an account, you will have to enter a new code. This code is generated by an application on your phone or computer. This application is called a TOTP client.

Why Zero-TOTP ?

Strong encryption

All your data is encrypted with AES256, a very strong encryption standard.

Only you can decrypt your data with your password.

Zero-knowledge encryption

Zero-TOTP use zero-knowledge encryption with means than no one can access your data except you. Even the server administrator can't access your data. You and only you are able to decrypt your vault.

Your are the key holder

Only your passphrase can decrypt your vault. All the encryption is performed in your browser, and your data is safe before it has even left your device !

100% open-source

Zero-TOTP is 100% open source and 100% free. Never trust anyone but you. We are 100% transparent and anyone can access to the source code

About Zero-Knowledge Encryption

Zero Knowledge Encryption (ZKE) refers to a robust security measure that ensures the utmost protection of your data. With ZKE, your information is encrypted locally on your device before being uploaded to the cloud, and only you hold the encryption keys, guaranteeing that no one, including the service provider, can access your data without your explicit permission. This empowers you with complete control over your sensitive information while enjoying the convenience and flexibility of cloud storage.

The project
